About PoinTester

PoinTester is a cross platform based beta testing application where you will be able to sign and link up accounts towards your PSN/GamerTags/Wii accounts and other alternatives to connect your gaming accounts towards and beta test upcoming games where you will be able to score reward points to win in-game items, DLC Packs and better yet, the whole game itself! The purpose of this application is to engage users more towards beta testing. The more beta testers, the faster, and more updated and excellent game content for upcoming release. Rankings

Ranking systems are based upon an active user and depending how ofter they beta test other upcoming games. The way a user can score points is by leveling up their user profiles. Depending what level the user is, users will be ranked depending on what level they are. For example if a user is level 32, he will be ranked as an "Internship". Players are able to earn points depending what accomplishments they've done between games they've been playing. The more accomplishments, the higher the levels, the better the ranks!

Ranking System

  • 10-20: Beginner
